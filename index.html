Who I am.

Hi. I am a freelancer and entrepreneur based in the Montreal, Quebec area. I have a burning passion for software design, the Web and security. I hold the deep belief that programming is an art form, and that it should be treated as such.

In my free time, you can usually find me cycling through the hills surrounding our beautiful island, hiking in the wild, or weaving tapestries of code in the small cafes downtown.
